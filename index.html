Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 21, 2025

Thank you for using Invoice Generator.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or data. All information you enter (such as company name, client details, or invoice items) stays on your device and is used only to generate the invoice you create.

2. Data Storage

No data is stored on our servers. All processing happens locally in your browser. Once you close or refresh the page, all entered data is lost unless you choose to save the generated invoice file yourself.

3. Permissions

The app does not request any special permissions, such as access to your contacts, files, or location.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

5. Cookies

Our app does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

6. Security

Since no personal data is transmitted or stored, there are no security risks related to user data within this app. However, always ensure you download invoices securely and store them safely on your own device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
